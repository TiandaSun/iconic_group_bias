## 6. Uses and Limitations

* **Intended use.** Benchmark for evaluating per-group recognition accuracy of Chinese ethnic minority costumes by vision-language models. Research-only use.
* **Known limitations.** (a) Potential Western-tourist-photographer bias (many source photos may originate from tourism and cultural-performance contexts rather than daily wear). (b) Images may over-represent performative/festival attire rather than everyday costume. (c) No age/gender balance is controlled for. (d) Only 5 of 55 officially recognised Chinese ethnic minorities are covered.
